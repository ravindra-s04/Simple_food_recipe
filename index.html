Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

For the Jamuns:

  • 1 cup khoya (mawa), grated
  • All-purpose Flour (Maida): 2 tablespoons
  • Baking Powder: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Cardamom Powder: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Milk: As needed to form a dough

For the Sugar Syrup:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • Water: 1.5 cups
  • Cardamom Pods: 4-5, crushed
  • Rose Water: 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Lemon Juice: 1 teaspoon

Process:

Prepare Dough:

  1. Mix: Crumble 1 cup khoya with 2 tbsp flour, 1/4 tsp baking powder, and 1/4 tsp cardamom powder.
  2. Knead: Add milk as needed to form a soft dough. Rest for 10-15 minutes
  3. Shape: Divide dough and roll into smooth, crack-free balls

Make Sugar Syrup:

  1. Heat Oil: Heat ghee/oil on medium-low.
  2. Fry: Fry balls until golden brown, about 6-8 minutes. Drain on paper towels.

Barfi

Ingredients:

Basic Barfi:

  • Khoya (Mawa): 2 cups, crumbled
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Cardamom Powder: 1/2 teaspoon

For Greasing:

  • Ghee or Butter: As needed

Optional Garnishes:

  • Chopped Nuts: Almonds, pistachios, cashews
  • Saffron Strands
  • Edible Silver Leaf (Varak)

Process:

  1. Cook Khoya: Heat 2 cups khoya in a non-stick pan over medium heat
  2. Add Sugar: Stir in 1 cup sugar; cook until the mixture thickens and leaves the pan sides.
  3. Add Cardamom: Mix in 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  4. Pour & Set: Grease a tray with ghee, pour the mixture, and spread evenly
  5. Garnish: Add chopped nuts, saffron, or silver leaf if desired.
  6. Cool & Cut: Let it cool for 1-2 hours, then cut into pieces.
